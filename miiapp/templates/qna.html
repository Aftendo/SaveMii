Questions and Answers

Q: How do you archive NNIDs?

A: Basically, we fetch NNID information from the Nintendo API. Then, we download every Mii image in the images key and we save the NNID data in a row to the database.

Q: Why is it named like the tool to extract savedata from the Wii U?

A: (Rixy) I didn't knew that this SaveMii existed lmao

Q: Someone archived my NNID but I want it deleted. How do I do this?

A: First, register to the website. Then, go to your NNID's page and click the "Complaint" button and write your complaint, with a URL to a photo of your NNID settings with your Mii and NNID clearly visible. Then, wait for a few days, and if everything worked, your NNID should be deleted.

Q: I sent a complaint, but so far my NNID hasn't been deleted; why?

A: There could be multiple reasons for this:

  • Nobody looked at your complaint for now. We have lives, and we may not respond to your complaint. Just wait for a few days.
  • Your complaint wasn't convicing enough: missing photo, NNID and/or Mii not clearly visible.
